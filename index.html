History & Legacy

Royal Heritage

Pithla (or Peethla) is a historic village in Jaisalmer district, Rajasthan, located approximately 26 km south of Jaisalmer city. It has been a significant stronghold of the Rawlot Bhati Rajputs.

Royal Lineage

Maharawal Sabal Singh (Maharawal of Jaisalmer, 1651–1661) granted Pithla Thikana to his son. Thakur Rawal Bankidas Singh became the first ruler of Pithla Thikana, marking the beginning of the Rawlot branch from here.

Geographical Significance

Mesmerizing Sunset at Pithla Thikana

Pithla is renowned for its high-quality yellow sandstone, which has been used in historic structures across Rajasthan. The village is geographically significant due to its close proximity to Jaisalmer and its contribution to Rajasthan's architectural heritage.
